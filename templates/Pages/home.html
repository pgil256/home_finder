Built as an analytics portfolio piece

Not a listing search. A transparent market-analysis workflow.

Pinellas Market Lens uses official public records to demonstrate the full data path: ingestion, normalization, database-backed filtering, pandas/numpy aggregation, Chart.js visualization, and export generation.

It is exploratory analysis, not a valuation model or investment-advice engine. The goal is to make market structure visible and auditable.
