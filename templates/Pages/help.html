How It Works

Turn your property search criteria into comprehensive reports in three simple steps.

Select Your Keywords

Choose the search criteria that matter most to you: cities, ZIP codes, property types, and price ranges.

  • Click "Search Properties" to begin
  • Pick keywords relevant to your search
  • Drag to rank by importance
1
2

Complete the Search Form

Based on your keywords, a customized form appears. Fill in the specific values you're looking for.

  • Enter specific cities or ZIP codes
  • Set price range and property type
  • Hit submit and let us do the rest

Download Your Reports

Once processing completes, you'll receive three downloadable documents with your property data.

  • Visual charts summarizing the market
  • PDF listing of matched properties
  • Excel spreadsheet with full data
3

What You'll Get

Market Visuals

Charts and graphs showing price distributions, property counts, and market trends for your search area.

PDF Report

A formatted document listing each property with key details: address, value, size, and tax information.

Excel Spreadsheet

Full dataset with all property details. Filter, sort, and analyze the data however you need.

Property Appraiser

Values, ownership, building details

Tax Collector
