Getting Started

From search to reports in minutes.

Turn your property search criteria into comprehensive, downloadable reports in three simple steps.

The Process
1

Select Keywords

Choose what matters to you: cities, ZIP codes, property types, and price ranges.

  • Click "Search Properties" to begin
  • Pick relevant search criteria
  • Drag to rank by importance
2

Complete the Form

A customized form appears based on your keywords. Fill in the specific values you want.

  • Enter specific cities or ZIPs
  • Set price range & property type
  • Hit submit and we'll do the rest
3

Download Reports

Once processing completes, download three comprehensive documents with your property data.

  • Visual market analysis charts
  • PDF listing of matched properties
  • Excel spreadsheet with full data
