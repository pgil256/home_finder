Help

Welcome to the Help and Support page. Here, you'll find guidance on the workflow of how to turn your real estate queries into graphics and data.

Enter Keywords

To start generating excels:

  1. Click on the "Find Real Estate" link.
  2. Choose the keywords that are most relevant to your property search.
  3. Rank them in order from most to least important, then submit.

Complete the Form

After selecting and ranking your key words:

  1. A form will be generated based on your input.
  2. Fill the form based on the types of listing you want.
  3. Hit submit! We'll take care of the rest.

Reviewing your documents

Once you submit your form:

  1. Real estate websites will be searched, and your data will be gathered.
  2. Once this is completed, you will receive 3 downloadable documents:
        Visual data summarizing your search
        A listing of your collected data
        A spreadsheet providing your aggregated data

Additional Support

If you encounter any issues or have questions, please don't hesitate to contact me via email for any issues or ideas for feature implementation.
