The Mission

Making property data accessible to everyone.

A free tool built to help navigate the Pinellas County real estate market by consolidating public property data into one searchable interface.

"

I created Home Finder because I saw how difficult it was for people to navigate the home-buying process in today's economy.

Property data is scattered across multiple county websites, each with different interfaces and search capabilities. This tool brings it all together into one place, making it easier to research properties, compare values, and understand tax obligations before making one of life's biggest decisions.

Core Values

Free Forever

This project will never be monetized. It exists purely to help people find homes, not to profit from the housing crisis.

Educational Project

Built as a learning project and to help others. Check out the code, suggest improvements, or fork it for your own county.

Official Data Sources

All data comes directly from Pinellas County Property Appraiser and Tax Collector websites. No third-party scraping.

No Tracking

Your searches are yours. No analytics, no user tracking, no selling your data. Just a tool that helps you find homes.
